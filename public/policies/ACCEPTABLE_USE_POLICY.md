# Acceptable Use Policy

**Effective Date:** February 7, 2026

This Acceptable Use Policy ("Policy") outlines the acceptable use of Islakayd's equipment rental marketplace platform (the "Service"). By using the Service, you agree to comply with this Policy. Violation may result in suspension or termination of your account.

## 1. Prohibited Activities

You agree not to use the Service to:

### 1.1 Illegal Activities
- Engage in any illegal activities or promote illegal content
- Violate intellectual property rights of others
- Conduct fraudulent transactions or scams

### 1.2 Harmful Content
- Post or share content that is defamatory, obscene, or offensive
- Distribute malware, viruses, or harmful code
- Harass, threaten, or intimidate other users

### 1.3 System Abuse
- Attempt to gain unauthorized access to our systems or other users' accounts
- Use automated tools, bots, or scrapers without permission
- Overload our servers with excessive requests
- Circumvent security measures or access restrictions

### 1.4 Misuse of Service
- Create fake accounts or impersonate others
- Manipulate ratings, reviews, or search results
- Use the Service for commercial purposes not related to equipment rental
- Share account credentials with others

## 2. Content Standards

### 2.1 Equipment Listings
- All listings must accurately describe the equipment
- Photos must be authentic and not misleading
- Prohibited items include illegal, dangerous, or restricted equipment

### 2.2 User Communications
- Respect other users' privacy
- Do not spam or send unsolicited messages
- Use appropriate language in all communications

## 3. Equipment Rental Guidelines

### 3.1 Safety and Compliance
- Ensure all equipment meets safety standards
- Comply with local laws and regulations regarding equipment use
- Provide proper training or instructions when necessary

### 3.2 Rental Terms
- Honor all booking agreements
- Maintain equipment in good condition
- Report any issues promptly

## 4. Monitoring and Enforcement

We reserve the right to:
- Monitor user activity for compliance
- Remove content that violates this Policy
- Suspend or terminate accounts for violations
- Report illegal activities to authorities
