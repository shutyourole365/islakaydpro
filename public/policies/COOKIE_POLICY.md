## 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us provide a better user experience by remembering your preferences and understanding how you use our Service.

## 2. Types of Cookies We Use

These are necessary for the Service to function properly. They include:

- Session cookies to maintain your browsing session

### 2.2 Performance Cookies

### 2.3 Functional Cookies
These enhance your experience:

### 2.4 Marketing Cookies
Used for targeted advertising (only with your consent):

## 4. How to Control Cookies

### 4.1 Browser Settings
You can control cookies through your browser settings:
- **Chrome**: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies
- **Firefox**: Preferences > Privacy & Security > Cookies
- **Safari**: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data

### 4.2 Our Cookie Preferences
Within the Service, you can manage cookie preferences through our cookie banner or settings page.

### 4.3 Opt-Out
- For Google Analytics: Visit [Google Analytics Opt-out](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)
- For other third-party cookies: Refer to their respective privacy policies

## 5. Cookie Retention

- Session cookies expire when you close your browser
- Persistent cookies remain until deleted or expired (typically 1-2 years)
- We regularly review and update our cookie usage
